Boot Execution & BroadCastRecievers

* What is BroadCast Recievers ?
  ---
  -> Its broadcaster that can works on Events send by Apps
     If your app sends an event then other apps can catch this event via BroadcastRecievers

* Booting :
  ---
  When android boots up It send 1 event in Broadcadt Recievers
  (ie that this android device boots started & completes so you can perform your work that you need to
      once android device boots up
      So this way apps can do some initial stuffs they want when android device boots completes

      App can do some initiation of Service that the wanted !)
